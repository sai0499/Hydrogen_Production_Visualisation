About the App

PEM Visualization brings proton exchange membrane processes to life with interactive 3D animations and in-depth theoretical insights. Built to support the Visualization of Process Engineering Applications course, it empowers learners to explore complex process steps intuitively.

Course Project

This project was completed for the "Visualization of Process Engineering Applications" course, integrating simulation, 3D modelling, and interactive elements to reinforce engineering concepts.
